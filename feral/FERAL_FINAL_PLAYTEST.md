# Feral v4 consolidated playtest

Use a disposable copy of the current save. One Skeever claim should survive migration, while all old permanent Feral actor-value bonuses and the generic Feral Act disappear.

1. Load, wait 30 seconds, and open Feral. Confirm Status, Instincts, and Settings pages appear.
2. Confirm the claim-window slider defaults to 180 seconds and accepts 60/180/300. Kill a supported creature personally and verify the Status page shows its family and remaining time.
3. Claim once, verify a second cast fails, and confirm follower kills do not create a window.
4. Enable developer tools. For Wolf, set 2 claims and simulate one claim. Confirm rank 1 and exactly one **Feral Shape: Wolf** power.
5. Cast Wolf shape. Confirm its visible Active Effect, body morph, mark, 50% stats, shared Bodymorph lock, and natural cleanup after 120 seconds.
6. Repeat at 9→10 and 24→25 claims. Confirm power replacement and 75%/100% effects with no duplicate powers.
7. Use Return to Self during a shape. Confirm stats, Feral morph keys, mark, storage state, and the shared lock all clear.
8. While a Bodymorph Alterations form is active, confirm Feral shapes are blocked. While a Feral shape is active, confirm Bodymorph forms are blocked and its MCM names the Feral form.
9. Spot-check Bear, Spider, Mudcrab, Horse, and Troll silhouettes and marks. Verify each displays the correct combat benefit.
10. Enable Feral Path, simulate claims in each XP band, then disable it. Confirm 25/35/50 XP awards and exact Experience restoration.
11. Disable Feral while transformed. Confirm cleanup occurs and powers disappear while ranks persist. Re-enable and confirm powers return.
12. Save/reload and verify ranks, settings, powers, claim timer, Experience state, and an empty shared transformation lock.
